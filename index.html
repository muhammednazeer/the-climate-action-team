About Us

Our main goal is to protect environment

The Climate Action Team Initiative is a non-governmental organization founded on September 25, 2019, to advocate for climate action and environmental sustainability. We are a group of passionate climate advocates who believe in collaborative approaches to combat climate change and empower communities.

  • Our Impact
  • Mission
  • Vision

Through our various initiatives and collaborations, we’ve been able to plant over 10,000 trees, keep children in school, and educate communities about sustainable practices.

At the Climate Action Team, our mission is clear: to influence policy, empower communities, and protect the environment. We work to bring awareness to climate issues, advocate for children’s rights, and help build leadership skills among young girls and boys.

Get involved today by volunteering, donating, or partnering with us. Help us create a greener, safer world for future generations.

Our Initiatives

Empowering Communities and Protecting Our Planet

Befriend a Tree

Encouraging communities to plant and care for fruit trees, this initiative provides both sustenance and economic benefit by offering a sustainable source of food and income.

Plastics for Knowledge

Through recycling plastics, this initiative helps underprivileged children stay in school, promoting education and environmental stewardship.

Climate is No Joke

Raising awareness on climate action, we conduct seminars and school visits to engage communities and inspire proactive environmental care.

PeaceWorks4

Partnering with organizations to advocate for peace, this initiative connects peaceful resolutions with environmental sustainability in insecure regions.
